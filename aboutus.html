About Us

FemPrish: Empowering Equality in India. FemPrish is a non-profit organization challenging negative perceptions of feminism. Focusing on India, they promote gender equality, combat violence, and improve access to education and healthcare. Through advocacy and collaboration, FemPrish strives for a more inclusive society where everyone can thrive.

Femprish

Femprish is an organisation that focuses on advocating women’s rights and gender equality. We want to get rid of the negative connotation that comes with the ‘feminist’, and the stigma that accompanies the talk of women’s right. We want to change the world step by step, because every revolution starts with one strong voice.
